This is Kuala Lumpur's official tourism website. It is a one-stop travel information portal that offers you the best recommendations on places to stay, food to try and the fascinating things to do and see - all to experience the best of this vibrant and exciting city. In 2012, KL was the world's 6th most visited city by international tourists. In 2011, KL was awarded "Asia's Leading City Break Destination" by the World Travel Awards. In line with this, the Kuala Lumpur City Hall (KLCH) has initiated several programmes to further spur its growth in the global tourism sector.

This website provides links to enable you to make direct bookings with a host of service providers, including tour operators, travel services, accommodations and even venues & attractions. In addition, the KLCH has produced a number of publications that include street directories, maps and brochures that are made available for free at many public places. With all these information conveniently available, we hope that you will enjoy your trip to KL and ultimately, be charmed by its friendly people and captivating attractions.
